# Privacy Policy for Quick Fingers

Last updated: March 15, 2026

## Overview
Quick Fingers is a color pattern matching puzzle game developed for iOS and Android. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Do Not Collect
Quick Fingers does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

## Data Stored on Your Device
Game progress, settings (theme, language, audio preferences) are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

## Advertising
Quick Fingers uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and identifiers to show relevant ads. This includes:
- Device advertising ID
- IP address
- Device information (OS version, device model)

For more information on how Google uses this data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalized ads, you can adjust your device settings:
- Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

## Children's Privacy
Quick Fingers is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
